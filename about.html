It’s all about Success Together

Seattle PMS has been established with the goal to combine various levels of expertise in the management and business aspects to provide the best possible Development and Project Management Service to our clients.

In Seattle PMS we have the abilities to research, develop, implement, manage and operate turn-key projects and solutions. In other terms, we manage business developments from inception through completion to intended use.

Our team is experienced in Project Management, Engineering, Information Technology, Financial and Marketing Management, which allow us to manage all facets of a project.

“In Seattle PMS we manage business developments from inception through completion to intended use.”

Our Vision

Revolutionizing the way we do projects and businesses by embracing innovative products and services hence great work experiences can be achieved

Our Mission

Building sustainable businesses in UAE through direct investment and partnership with reputed abroad companies, enabling our customers do better business using state of art technologies, and creating job opportunities for UAE locals as well as others

The UAE continued to attract foreign direct investment (FDI), with inflows of FDI reaching USD 10.9 billion in 2015, largely focused on construction, finance, and wholesale and retail trade. Therefore, Seattle Project Management Services (Seattle PMS) has been founded with a specific business model which is based on being a business enabling
entity between clients/customers and service providers, financial institutions and/or freelancers.
Seattle PMS role is to enable companies who are either in UAE or outside of UAE to succeed by providing them market knowledge and feasibility, regulation understanding, financial requirements and fund raising.

Words from the Chairman

- Eng. Gamal El Sadat

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) maintains a position as the major trade and investment hub for a large geographic region, which includes not only the Middle East and North Africa, but also South Asia, Central Asia, and Africa. According to the 2017 Investment Climate Statements Report that was issued by The Bureau of Economic and Business Affairs, U.S. Department of State, UAE ranked 17th of 143 economies in the World Economic Forum’s 2015-2016 overall Global Competitiveness Index, and 31st of 189 on the World Bank’s 2016 Ease of Doing Business report.

Multinational companies cite the UAE’s political and economic stability, rapid population and Gross Domestic Product (GDP) growth, and fast-growing capital markets as positive factors contributing to the UAE’s attractiveness to foreign investors.
